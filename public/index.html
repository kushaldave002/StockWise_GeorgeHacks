The Problem We're Solving

Food Deserts in DC

Over 33% of residents in Wards 7 and 8 live more than a mile from a full-service grocery store. Corner stores are their lifeline — but they can't stock fresh food without knowing there's demand.

StockWise Closes the Gap

We aggregate real demand from residents, connect it to store inventory and DCCK's supply chain, and make sure the right food gets to the right neighborhood. SNAP-friendly, community-driven.
